Background

NHS Investment

NHS organisations invested clinician time in the development of open-eObs. Now all healthcare organisations can benefit from that investment.

Open Source

NHS England uses public money to fund open source projects. Open source protects the NHS from lock-in and guarantees patient benefit before private profit.

Community

Clinical Leadership

open-eObs is clinically led by NHS Health and Social Care Professionals with support from The Apperta Foundation, a not-for-profit community interest company funded by NHS England Code4Health and Open Source Programmes.

Open Source

open-eObs is a module that integrates with Odoo, a suite of web-first open source enterprise apps. Open-eObs can be used as stand-alone application, yet is will also integrate seamlessly with other modules opening up a wide range of additional features.
